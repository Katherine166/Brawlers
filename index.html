Best Brawlers for Every Game Mode in Nulls Brawl

Best Brawlers for Gem Grab

Gem Grab is all about teamwork, holding control of the gems, and ensuring your team’s success. The key to excelling in this mode is having brawlers that can deal damage, protect the gems, and control the map.

Top Brawlers for Gem Grab

For Gem Grab, brawlers like Jessie, Brock, Pam, and Poco are some of the best choices. Jessie’s turret provides excellent map control, while Brock’s long-range damage can pick off enemies before they even get close to the gems. Pam offers both offense and defense with her healing ability and high damage, making her perfect for holding the gem mine. Poco’s healing power can keep teammates alive during intense battles, which is essential for keeping control over the gems.

Why These Brawlers Shine

These brawlers are effective because they combine damage output with abilities that support the team. Jessie’s turret can be used to keep enemies at bay while you gather gems. Brock’s damage from afar helps clear out enemy brawlers trying to interfere with gem collection. Pam and Poco both help their teams stay alive longer, making sure that the team doesn’t lose control of their gems. In Gem Grab, it’s not just about getting the gems but also protecting them—and these brawlers do just that.

Best Brawlers for Showdown

Showdown is a mode where it’s every brawler for themselves. To succeed here, you need a brawler who can survive, outlast enemies, and deal damage effectively.

Top Brawlers for Showdown

In Showdown, Rosa, Leon, and Shelly are excellent choices. Rosa’s shielding ability and high survivability allow her to tank damage and last longer against other players. Leon’s invisibility and burst damage make him deadly in solo play, allowing him to pick off enemies while staying hidden. Shelly’s strong burst damage and ability to heal herself give her the edge in one-on-one fights.

Why These Brawlers Are Effective

What makes these brawlers effective in Showdown is their ability to outlast opponents. Rosa can take hits without going down quickly, Leon can surprise enemies by using his invisibility to strike first, and Shelly can use her strong offensive abilities to eliminate enemies quickly. In Showdown, survival and surprise are key, and these Brawlers excel at both.

Best Brawlers for Bounty

In Bounty, strategy and control are paramount. You need brawlers who can deal damage from a distance and stay alive long enough to collect stars for your team.

Top Brawlers for Bounty

Piper, Rico, and Colt are ideal for Bounty. Piper’s long-range damage can hit enemies from afar, making her perfect for picking off opponents before they get too close. Rico’s ability to bounce his shots off walls gives him great map control, while Colt’s precise shots allow him to clear enemies effectively from a distance.

Why These Brawlers Excel

Brawlers like Piper, Rico, and Colt excel in Bounty because of their ability to deal damage from a distance while avoiding direct confrontation. Their long-range abilities let them secure stars without putting themselves at great risk. They’re also good at staying out of range while eliminating enemies, allowing them to keep collecting stars for their team.

Best Brawlers for Heist

Heist requires you to break through the enemy’s safe while defending your own. You need brawlers that can deal significant damage to the safe and stop the enemy from doing the same.

Top Brawlers for Heist

Dynamike, Bo, and El Primo are all fantastic picks for Heist. Dynamike’s ability to deal explosive damage at a distance makes him great for both attacking and defending. Bo can use his mines to trap enemies, while his long-range attack is perfect for damaging the safe. El Primo can deal massive damage up close and has the durability to withstand hits while attacking the enemy’s safe.

Why These Brawlers Work Well

The key to winning in Heist is damaging the enemy’s safe while also defending your own. Dynamike and Bo offer a good balance of offensive and defensive capabilities, while El Primo is excellent at getting up close and personal to break through the safe while withstanding damage. These brawlers can dish out significant damage while protecting themselves from enemies, making them perfect for this mode.

Best Brawlers for Siege

Siege is all about collecting bolts to power up a robot that helps you attack the enemy’s base. You need brawlers that can both collect bolts and deal with the enemy robot effectively.

Top Brawlers for Siege

Barley, Tick, and Sandy are the best options in Siege. Barley and Tick are great for controlling the battlefield with their area damage abilities, preventing enemies from gathering bolts. Sandy can control the battlefield with her sandstorm ability and provide support with her healing.

Why These Brawlers Are Great

In Siege, positioning is everything. Barley, Tick, and Sandy offer map control, either through area damage or crowd control. Barley and Tick can prevent the enemy from getting to the bolts or from attacking the robot effectively, while Sandy’s ability to heal and control the battlefield makes her invaluable. These brawlers are essential for both defense and offense, ensuring your team is always in control of the match.

Best Brawlers for Hot Zone

In Hot Zone, holding control over the zones is crucial, and only the brawlers who can provide area control or sustain themselves in combat will thrive. It’s a mode where positioning and area control are essential to victory.

Top Brawlers for Hot Zone

Frank, Tara, and Sprout are top-tier brawlers for Hot Zone. Frank’s stun ability can clear enemies out of the zone, giving his team time to dominate the area. Tara’s super, which pulls enemies together, can also help in crowd control, allowing her to either secure kills or push enemies out of the zone. Sprout’s ability to block off zones with his walls and control the space makes him a perfect choice for this mode.

Why These Brawlers Stand Out

These brawlers shine in Hot Zone because of their ability to control space and deny enemies access to key areas. Frank’s high health and stun make him great for holding the zone and making it difficult for opponents to approach. Tara’s super allows for strategic control over enemies, and Sprout’s ability to manipulate the battlefield with his walls provides excellent zoning and crowd control. In Hot Zone, you need to maintain your ground, and these brawlers excel at doing just that, either through control or healing.

Best Brawlers for Knockout

Knockout is a fast-paced mode where eliminations are key. To win, you need brawlers who can quickly take down enemies and outlast the competition in each round.

Top Brawlers for Knockout

For Knockout, Mortis, Edgar, and Byron are ideal. Mortis excels at quick eliminations with his fast mobility and ability to avoid damage. Edgar’s high burst damage and jump ability make him a menace in close-quarters combat, able to finish off enemies swiftly. Byron, on the other hand, offers utility with his healing and poison attacks, allowing him to both support teammates and finish off weakened opponents.

Why These Brawlers Work Well

These brawlers work best in Knockout because they can either eliminate opponents quickly or ensure they survive longer. Mortis can dive in, grab a quick kill, and escape, while Edgar's burst damage and mobility make him perfect for finishing off opponents quickly. Byron’s versatility ensures that he can both deal damage and heal his team when needed. In a mode like Knockout, where every elimination counts, these brawlers excel at quickly shifting the balance of power in your favor.

Best Brawlers for Trio Modes

Trio modes focus on teamwork, and having brawlers who work well with others can make all the difference. The best brawlers for this mode are those that complement their teammates with healing, synergy, and strong damage output.

Top Brawlers for Trio Modes

Nita, Poco, and Crow are fantastic for Trio. Nita offers good damage and her summonable bear helps with both offense and defense. Poco’s healing capabilities make him invaluable in keeping the team alive, especially during tough fights. Crow provides high mobility and poison damage, which is great for chipping away at opponents while helping control the flow of battle.

Why These Brawlers Are Great for Team Play

These brawlers are great for Trio because they bring out the best in their team. Nita’s bear helps in holding the enemy back, while her high damage output supports the team’s attack. Poco is the perfect healer, keeping the trio alive with his area healing abilities, while Crow’s poison damage can disrupt the enemy's plans, especially when paired with other damage dealers. Teamwork and synergy are the keys to victory in Trio modes, and these brawlers bring excellent value to any team. They combine strong damage with supportive abilities to create a balanced, effective force in battle.
